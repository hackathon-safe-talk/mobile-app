SafeTalk Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SafeTalk is a privacy-focused, on-device cybersecurity application. This Privacy Policy explains what data SafeTalk accesses, why it is needed, and how that data is handled when you use the application.

1. Data We Access

SafeTalk may access the following data only for security analysis on your device:

  • SMS messages — to identify phishing, scam, and malicious message patterns.
  • Notifications — to analyze threats from supported apps such as Telegram and similar sources.
  • URLs and file names — to detect suspicious links, harmful files, or dangerous attachment names.

2. On-Device Processing

SafeTalk performs its primary analysis directly on your device using local logic, rule-based detection, and on-device intelligence mechanisms.

Message content is not transmitted to external servers. The core analysis of supported messages and notifications is performed locally on your device.

3. Data Usage

Data accessed by SafeTalk is used only to provide security analysis, threat detection, warnings, and user-facing explanations about suspicious content.

4. Data Sharing

SafeTalk does not sell, rent, or share your message content with third parties. SafeTalk is not designed to transmit the analyzed message content to external servers as part of its primary protection workflow.

5. Permissions

SafeTalk may request permissions such as:

  • SMS access — to analyze incoming SMS messages for scam and phishing indicators.
  • Notification access — to inspect supported app notifications for suspicious activity.

These permissions are requested only to enable the corresponding protection features. If you deny or disable them, related protection modules may not function.

6. Local Storage and History

SafeTalk may store limited analysis history and related risk summaries locally on your device so that you can review previous results and statistics.

This data remains under your device control and may be removed through app controls or app deletion.

7. Security Limitations

SafeTalk is an advisory security tool. While it is designed to improve user safety, it does not guarantee 100% detection accuracy.

False positives and false negatives may occur. Users should always apply their own judgment before opening links, files, or responding to suspicious messages.

8. Children’s Privacy

SafeTalk is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because the application primarily performs local analysis on-device, the app does not intentionally gather and transmit children’s personal content as part of its main workflow.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, the updated version may be reflected inside the application or on this page.

10. Contact

For official SafeTalk information, please refer to the project’s published channels and repository.
