### 3.3 Imbalance & Conclusion
- **Dominant Language**: English heavily dominates both the combined total and the active V9 production dataset (~67% in V9).

- **Target Audience Match**: The target audience is presumably Uzbekistan. Having ~23% Uzbek vs ~67% English in V9 presents a high risk of poor performance on local threats. It relies too heavily on English global patterns (which may genericize intent) rather than local language nuances.
- **Risk of Bias**: The model is likely biased towards English spam structures and might miss nuanced Uzbek social engineering attacks, especially if local vernacular or mixed Cyrillic/Latin is used with novel text patterns.
